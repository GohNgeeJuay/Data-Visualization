World Happiness

Exploration of happiness worldwide and factors affecting happiness

What is the World Happiness Report?

World Hapiness Report is an annual landmark survey of the state of happiness in countries worldwide.

It measures how happy people perceive themselves to be. This survey is conducted by Gallup World Poll.

Human Development Index and Happiness

Human Development Index (HDI) is a statistic for assessing countries' development based on a more comprehensive approach compared to economic factors alone. The values can range between 0 and 1. Higher values means higher development. These factors for HDI are:.

  • Health (measured by life expectancy)
  • Education (measured by mean years of schooling and expected years of schooling)
  • Standard of Living (measured by Gross National Income (PPP$))

Does HDI affect Happiness? Look to the right and find out.

There seems to be a positive correlation! Happier countries usually have higher HDI.

GINI and Happiness

GINI is a statistic for assessing countries' income distribution. It ranges between 0 and 1, with 0 being total equality and 1 being total inequality.

Do countries with more income inquality more unhappy? Look to the right and find out.

There seems to be a positive correlation! Happier countries usually have more income equality.

Population Growth and Happiness

Population Growth is the annual changes for the population of a country. It is measured as the change in the population of a country divided by the total population of the country. It is measured as a percentage.

World Happiness Report Statistic

The happiness is scored based on respondents ratings. The ratings are comprised by a number of metrics which include:.

  • GDP per capita
  • Family
  • Freedom
  • Generosity
  • Health
  • Trust in Government
  • Dystopia and Residual

Dystopia is the worst possible country to live in (in terms of all of the above factors). Respondents were ask to rate their happiness in comparison to it. Dystopia serves as a benchmark against which all countries can be favorably compared to.

Residuals are the unexplained components of the happiness score.
